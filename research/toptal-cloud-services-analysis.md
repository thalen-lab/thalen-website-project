5. **DevOps Services**

6. **Cloud Security Services**

8. **Azure Consulting**

## Key Content Patterns

### Messaging Approach
- **Benefit-focused:** Emphasizes business outcomes over technical features
- **Trust-building:** Shows verified experts, years of experience, previous clients
- **Flexibility:** Multiple delivery models and engagement options
- **Comprehensive:** Full spectrum of cloud services from strategy to managed services

### Visual Elements
- **Icons:** Simple line icons for each service
- **Team Photos:** Real expert profiles with credentials
- **Client Logos:** Trust indicators (Okta, USC, Duolingo visible)
- **Organizational Charts:** Visual representation of team structure

### Content Hierarchy
1. Hero with clear value proposition
2. Service offerings grid (browsable catalog)
3. Team capabilities and expertise
4. Process and engagement model
5. Thought leadership and credibility
6. Related services and cross-selling
