## Case Study 2: DoD Manufacturing Facility Achieves 99.2% Uptime

**Category:** Defense

**Challenge:**

A Department of Defense manufacturing facility faced frequent equipment downtime that disrupted production schedules and threatened mission-critical deadlines. Traditional reactive maintenance approaches were insufficient for the facility's complex operations. The facility needed predictive capabilities to prevent failures before they occurred.

**Results:**

## Case Study 3: VA Medical Center Integrates 12 Legacy Systems

**Category:** Federal Government

**Challenge:**

A Veterans Affairs medical center operated 12 separate legacy healthcare systems that didn't communicate with each other. Clinicians and administrators had to log into multiple systems to access patient information, creating inefficiencies and increasing the risk of errors. The fragmented systems created an administrative burden that took time away from patient care.

**Solution:**

NexDyne implemented a FedRAMP iPaaS (Integration Platform as a Service) solution that unified all 12 legacy systems under a single interface. The platform enables seamless data exchange between systems while maintaining HIPAA compliance and federal security standards. Staff can now access all necessary information through one portal without switching between applications.

**Results:**

- Successfully integrated 12 disparate healthcare systems
- Reduced administrative burden by 60% for 8,500 staff members
- Improved patient data accessibility and accuracy
- Maintained full HIPAA compliance throughout integration
- Decreased average patient check-in time by 40%

**Technologies:** Integration, HIPAA, FedRAMP
